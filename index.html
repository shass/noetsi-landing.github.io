Noetsi is a decentralized non-custodial lending platform which facilitates crypto-collateralized borrowing at fixed rates and terms without liquidations. Noetsi enables peer-to-peer lending and borrowing via smart-contracts, and a marketplace for buying and selling of existing outstanding loans. Our mission is to enable borrowers to obtain liquidity while maintaining exposure to their crypto assets, and offer sustainable and attractive yields to lenders.
