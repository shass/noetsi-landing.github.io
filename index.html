Noetsi is a decentralized non-custodial lending platform that facilitates crypto-collateralized borrowing at fixed rates and terms, with zero liquidation risk. As a core infrastructure layer for permissionless on-chain fixed-income instruments, we provide a platform enabling lending via smart-contracts and a marketplace for secondary liquidity. Our mission is to enable crypto-natives and financial institutions to obtain longer-term liquidity while maintaining exposure to crypto assets, while offering sustainable and attractive yields to lenders.
